# P5-ERROR-001 audit

## Decision

Blind error analysis is formally `BLOCKED_NO_RAW_OUTPUT`. No error label,
sample list, adjudication item, or representative case is produced without
raw predictions and a pre-registered sampling frame.

## Frozen contract

The taxonomy covers perception, state, mechanism, causal, temporal, physical,
uncertainty, tool, and format errors. Model identity is hidden during sampling;
post-hoc case selection is forbidden. Two-rater consistency and a disagreement
queue are required. Negative results must be retained.

Test access remains `NO_ACCESS_CONFIRMED`; no test/private asset was read.

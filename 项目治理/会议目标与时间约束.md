# Submission Target and Schedule Constraint

## Target venue

The project target venue is **ICLR 2027**.

## Mandatory planning rules

- Milestones, experiment freezes, paper-data exports, independent audits, and release preparation MUST be planned backward from the ICLR 2027 submission cycle.
- The target venue is a planning constraint, not evidence of acceptance or publication.
- Official ICLR 2027 deadlines, formatting rules, and submission requirements MUST be verified from official sources before they are used as fixed dates.
- Until the official schedule is verified, all schedule fields remain `TBD` and the project MUST use internal safety deadlines earlier than the external deadline.

## Scope impact

This constraint applies to the project charter, detailed experiment and paper plan, progress records, claims freeze, paper export, reproducibility audit, and final release checklist.
